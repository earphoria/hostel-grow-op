Will Rock Your Socks Off

Hostel Grow-Op is a network of neighbors and volunteers growing food in their homes and supporting the efforts of community gardens by sharing knowledge, resources, and time.

At our location in Logan Square, we host weekly garden days, all-ages workshops and do community composting for free.

Get involved!

We are currently seeking new volunteers who are interested in varying
degrees of involvement. Seeking:

  • Volunteer twice a month on our Tuesday garden work days (3p-8p March-October)
  • Volunteer twice a month on our Saturday community garden days (10a-3p
    March - October)
  • Volunteer at youth group/school garden days (dates TBA March-October)
  • Planning Committee Member: help with administrative work, event curation, fielding donations, volunteer coordinating, making planting/growing maps, and graphic design (meets monthly January - August)
  • Home Growers Program: Volunteer to start seeds for both your garden and for community gardens in your home. All supplies provided
  • Physical and financial donations

    ((( for volunteering, you will receive garden resources, indoor house plants, vegetable transplants for your garden, organic fertilizer, compost drop-offs, home vermi-composting set-ups, and more depending on your level of involvement )))

    Let us know if you'd be interested in scheduling an in-person or phone meeting to speak further. We're also happy to correspond with you via email.
